What is WastEradict

WastEradict is a web based platform that enable communities to effectively manage wastes and take care of the environment as the name itself suggest.


How It Works

Real-Time Notifications:

All users with a WastEradict account receive real-time notifications based on the garbage collectors' schedule in their area. This allows users to prepare their garbage in advance and be ready when the collectors arrive.


Easy Disposal:

Users can manage waste disposal with a few clicks. By logging into their accounts and pinning their garbage location on the dashboard map, garbage collectors will see it in real-time.


Efficeincy for Garbage Collectors:

With WastEradict, garbage collectors no longer need to follow the traditional door-to-door method to find people with waste to dispose of. This innovation saves them valuable time and energy, which are crucial resources.


Community Reporting:

Residents can report instances of irresponsible waste disposal to nearby government offices in charge of the environment, ensuring appropriate actions are taken.


Why WastEradict is Important

WastEradict is incredibly useful for everyone, from individuals to organizations and government bodies. Most importantly, it plays a vital role in helping the environment heal and thrive by making waste management more efficient and community-driven.
